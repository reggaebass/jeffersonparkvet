    Location

    Welcome to Jefferson Park Veterinary Clinic in West Sacramento!

    Our clinic has been serving West Sacramento since October of 2011. We aspire to provide a full range of caring, and compassionate, high quality veterinary services for your canine and feline companions.

    Dr. Mandell and our caring professional staff are dedicated to the well-being of your pet. As a board-certified specialist in clinical pathology, Dr. Mandell is committed to providing high-quality patient care and precise diagnoses for her patients. Clinical pathology involves the study and diagnosis of diseases, enabling us to uncover the underlying causes of illness. We look forward to working with you to keep your pet healthy and happy, now and for years to come.

    Our consultants, board certified veterinary radiologists and veterinary surgeons, cardiologist consult at our practice to provide their expert services on site.

    • Am I allowed to come in to the clinic with my pet?

      Unfortunately, clients are not allowed to come in to our facility. We will take great care of your pets when they are dropped off for their appointment.

    • My pet has an emergency, can I come in as a same day appointment?

      We usually have one or two same day appointment slots available every morning for emergency appointments in first-come-first-serve basis. We will do our very best to fit in your emergency on the same day, if our staffing and schedule allow.
